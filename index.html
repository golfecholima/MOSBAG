ABOUT

Opened in 1991, Miracle of Science stands strong as the leader in geek-chic. Its menu is a chalk art "periodic table of the elements". Miracle was one of the first restaurants to employ the open floor concept. This place is always packed with artists, musicians, geophysicists and computer gurus who know they don't have to go looking anywhere else for a good time.

Manager: Annie Park

Miracle of Science does not accept reservations. Seating is on a first come, first serve basis. Private parties may be accommodated during non-peak hours. Please contact us for more information.
